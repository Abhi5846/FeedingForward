About Us

We are pleased to introduce FeedingForward: Saving Food, Feeding Lives!FeedingForward is committed to reducing food waste and building a sustainable food system. In order to turn leftover food into satisfying dinners, our network links food companies, organisations, and individuals.We prevent perfectly fine food from going to waste by using our creative ideas and network of collaborators. By converting surplus into food for undernourished people, we are collectively altering the narrative.Come join this delicious revolution with us! Make every bite count by preventing food waste and promoting sustainability together. By working together, we can make sure that no one goes hungry and receives the nutrition they need in the future.
